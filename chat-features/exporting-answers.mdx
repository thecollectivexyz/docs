## Overview

NTS Vortex allows you to export AI-generated answers to PDF or Word documents. This is especially useful for:
- Creating SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) with the SOP Generator
- Documenting important AI responses
- Sharing AI-generated content with team members
- Creating reference materials from chat conversations

<Info>
Export functionality works in any chat, whether you're using a specific agent like the SOP Generator or having a regular conversation with AI.
</Info>

## Exporting to PDF

To export an AI answer as a PDF document:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new chat">
    Begin a new conversation or open an existing chat where you want to export an answer.
 
    <Frame caption="New Chat">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/chat-features/exporting-answers/new-chat.png" alt="PDF export link in chat" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send a message">
    Type your message and send it to the AI. Wait for the AI to generate a response.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click PDF export">
    After the AI generates its response, look for the **PDF export** button or icon near the AI's message.

    <Frame caption="PDF export button">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/chat-features/exporting-answers/pdf-export-button.png" alt="PDF export link in chat" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Right-click the generated link">
    A download link will be generated. Right-click on this link to open the context menu.

    <Frame caption="PDF export link">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/chat-features/exporting-answers/pdf-export-link.png" alt="PDF export link in chat" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the file">
    Select **Save Link As** (or **Save Target As** depending on your browser) from the context menu. Name the file and choose where you want to save the PDF file on your computer.
  
    <Frame caption="Name File">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/chat-features/exporting-answers/name-file.png" alt="PDF export link in chat" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The PDF will contain the AI's response formatted as a document, making it easy to share or archive important information.
</Tip>

## Exporting to Word Document

To export an AI answer as a Word document:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new chat">
    Begin a new conversation or open an existing chat where you want to export an answer.

    <Frame caption="New Chat">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/chat-features/exporting-answers/new-chat.png" alt="PDF export link in chat" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send a message">
    Type your message and send it to the AI. Wait for the AI to generate a response.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click DOC export">
    After the AI generates its response, look for the **DOC export** or **Word export** button or icon near the AI's message.

    <Frame caption="PDF export link">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/chat-features/exporting-answers/word-export-button.png" alt="PDF export link in chat" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download automatically">
    The Word document will download automatically to your default downloads folder. No additional steps needed!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
The Word document is ready to use and can be opened in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or any compatible word processor.
</Check>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When to export">
    Export AI answers when:
    - The content will be used as official documentation
    - You need to share the information with others
    - The response contains important procedures or policies
    - You want to archive the information for future reference
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="PDF vs Word">
    Choose PDF when:
    - You need a read-only document
    - Formatting must be preserved exactly
    - The document will be shared widely
    
    Choose Word when:
    - You need to edit the content further
    - You want to add additional sections
    - You'll be collaborating on the document
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review before exporting">
    Always review the AI's response before exporting to ensure:
    - The information is accurate
    - The content is complete
    - Any formatting looks correct
    - The response meets your needs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Export button not appearing">
    - Make sure the AI has finished generating its response
    - Refresh the page if the button doesn't appear
    - Check that you're looking near the AI's message, not your own
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="PDF link not working">
    - Try right-clicking the link again
    - Check your browser's download settings
    - Ensure pop-up blockers aren't interfering
    - Try a different browser if issues persist
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Word document not downloading">
    - Check your browser's download settings
    - Look in your default downloads folder
    - Ensure you have sufficient disk space
    - Check if your browser blocked the download
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
