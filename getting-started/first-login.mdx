## Before You Begin

To log in to NTS Vortex for the first time, you will need:

- Your NTS Vortex URL (provided by your IT team)
- Your administrator email address
- Your temporary password (sent via email)

<Warning>
If you haven't received your login credentials, contact your NTS IT administrator before proceeding.
</Warning>

## Logging In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open NTS Vortex">
    Open your web browser and navigate to your NTS Vortex URL.

    <Frame caption="NTS Vortex login page">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/login-page.png" alt="NTS Vortex login page with email and password fields" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter your administrator email address and temporary password, then click **Sign In**.

    <Frame caption="Entering login credentials">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/enter-credentials.png" alt="Login form with email and password filled in" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the dashboard">
    After setting your new password, you will see the main NTS Vortex dashboard. Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the interface.

    <Frame caption="NTS Vortex main dashboard after login">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/dashboard-overview.png" alt="Main dashboard showing sidebar navigation and chat area" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the Interface

The NTS Vortex interface has several key areas:

### Main Areas

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Sidebar** | Navigation menu for accessing chats, agents, knowledge base, and settings |
| **Chat Area** | The main conversation space where you interact with AI |
| **Model Selector** | Dropdown to choose which AI model to use |
| **New Chat Button** | Start a fresh conversation |
| **Settings** | Access admin settings and configuration |

## Accessing Admin Settings

As an administrator, you have access to additional settings not available to regular users.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Navigate to Admin Panel">
    Click on **Admin Panel** or **Admin Settings** to access administrator features.

    <Frame caption="Admin Panel link">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/admin-panel-link.png" alt="Admin Panel option in settings menu" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore admin options">
    The Admin Panel gives you access to:
    
    - User management
    - Model configuration
    - Knowledge base management
    - System settings

    <Frame caption="Admin Panel overview">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/admin-panel-overview.png" alt="Admin Panel showing various configuration options" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your First Chat

Try out NTS Vortex by starting your first conversation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Chat">
    Click the **New Chat** button in the sidebar (top left) to start a fresh conversation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a model">
    Use the model selector at the top to choose an AI model.

    <Frame caption="Model selector dropdown">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/model-selector.png" alt="Dropdown showing available AI models" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Type your message">
    Type a message in the input box at the bottom. Try something simple like:
    
    > "Hello! Can you tell me what you can help me with?"

    <Frame caption="Typing your first message">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/first-message.png" alt="Chat input with a typed message" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send and receive a response">
    Press **Enter** or click the send button. The AI will respond with information about its capabilities.

    <Frame caption="AI response">
      <img src="/images/placeholders/getting-started/ai-response.png" alt="Chat showing the AI's response" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
Congratulations! You've successfully logged in to NTS Vortex and completed your first chat. You're now ready to explore the admin features.
</Check>
